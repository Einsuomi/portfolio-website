### Notable Project: Hotel Booking Platforms
Scraped three booking sites (booking.com, expedia.com, hotels.com) without applying any filters to preserve native recommendation algorithms. Finding: booking.com surfaces highest average prices, hotels.com surfaces lowest prices and lowest ratings — suggesting distinct commercial strategies (premium vs. budget targeting).

### Notable Project: Medium Articles Analysis
Textual analysis of 337 ML/AI/data science articles — topic modeling + frequency analysis to map the landscape of public ML discourse. Methodologically interesting for anyone building a knowledge base in this space.
